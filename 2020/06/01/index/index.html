About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently an assistant professor in the School of Computing and Artificial Intelligence at Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU). I received my Ph.D. from New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT) under the supervision of Prof. Chase Wu and Prof. Qiang Tang. My research interests include:

  • Blockchain Techniques
  • Security and Privacy
  • Applied Cryptography
  • Provably Secure DAPPs
    • P2P Content Delivery Network (CDN)
    • Industrial Chain and Value Chain
    • Cyber Security
    • Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)
    • Decentralized Identity
